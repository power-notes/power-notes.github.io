## What I write about

I write about the things that actually matter when you're shipping real enterprise solutions on the Power Platform — not the happy-path demos. That means:

- **Architecture decisions** that hold up under real load and real business complexity
- **Dataverse deep-dives** into the parts the documentation glosses over
- **ALM patterns** that let a team work without stepping on each other
- **Extensibility trade-offs** — when low-code is the answer and when it becomes the problem

Every post follows a problem-solution structure with real code, real trade-offs, and an honest take on what breaks at scale.
