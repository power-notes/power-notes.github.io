## Business Doesn't Wait  
*The truth no university teaches*

In the real world, a business need does not sit patiently while a development team builds authentication, a database schema, a frontend, a workflow engine, and a notification system from the ground up.

The business moves. Requirements shift. Competitors ship.  
**Time is the one resource that cannot be recovered.**

### What a typical business requirement looks like

- **Forms and data capture** — structured input with validation  
- **Approvals and routing** — workflows across teams  
- **Notifications** — Teams or email alerts  
- **Dashboards** — real-time visibility for management  
- **Audit trail** — full history of actions  
- **All of the above — quickly**

> **Business reality:**  
> This is not a “software project request.”  
> It’s a request for a **working solution — fast**.
